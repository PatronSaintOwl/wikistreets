Wikistreets

A tool to map street and sidewalk issues.

Features

  • Perfect for use on the road
  • Open to anyone with a phone or computer
  • Drop markers anywhere you find a dangerous issue with a street or sidewalk
  • Snap and save photos and details of the issue to the map
  • Share with others in your community
  • Advocate for improvements
  • Data is downloadable in standard KML format to import into other GIS applications
